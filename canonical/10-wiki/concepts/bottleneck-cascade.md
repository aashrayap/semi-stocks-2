`NVDA`, `CRWV`, `TSM`, `ASML`, `GOOGL`, `AMZN`.

## Active Sequence

| Stage | Status | Core Tickers |

| GPU cloud demand | Active | CRWV, NVDA, GOOGL, AMZN |
| N3 logic wafers | Active | TSM, NVDA, GOOGL, AMZN |

| EUV tools | Next | ASML, TSM, NVDA |

## Investor Alignment

- Baker: strongest direct exposure to `NVDA`, plus `GOOGL` and `AMZN`, and a smaller `CRWV` position.
- Leopold: concentrated `CRWV` exposure and active-name exits in `NVDA` and `TSM`.
