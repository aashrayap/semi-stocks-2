Bottleneck Cascade Relevance

CoreWeave sits at the intersection of nearly every active bottleneck in README:

  • GPU cloud (primary): Backlog 4x’d, capacity sold out through Aug-Sep 2026. Validates that compute demand is systematically underestimated.
  • N3 logic: 50K GB200s in one quarter. $30-35B capex = enormous chip demand. Directly pressures TSMC N3 capacity.
  • Memory: Every GPU needs HBM. CRWV’s GPU purchases are a major source of memory demand.
  • Pluggable optics: Every NVL72 rack uses OSFP transceivers. 100K+ GPUs deployed = significant COHR/LITE revenue.
  • Power: 260 MW added in Q4. 3.1 GW contracted. Building data centers is the physical constraint.

Why the Stock Sold Off

Despite $5.13B revenue (+168%), the market punished:

  1. Q1 guide of $1.9-2.0B missed Street’s $2.3B by $300M+
  2. EPS -$0.89 vs estimate -$0.21 (interest expense more than doubled)
  3. 2026 capex of $30-35B — spending $2.60 for every $1 of revenue
  4. Debt/equity 6.4x with no clear path to FCF positive near-term

The bull case: backlog is $66.8B with 5-year avg contract length, demand is “insatiable,” and cost of capital is declining (investment-grade GPU financing at ~5.9%). The bear case: this is a leveraged bet on AI demand persistence — if demand softens even slightly, the debt load becomes crushing.
